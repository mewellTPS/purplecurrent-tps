About Purple Current

Purple Current is a licensed commercial, industrial, and municipal electrical contractor serving Tampa Bay businesses. We handle large-scale electrical projects: new construction, tenant improvements, retrofits, maintenance, and emergency power systems.

Our Story

Why We Started Purple Current

Our team started as a branch of ACE Septic & Waste, installing and maintaining electrical systems for lift stations across Tampa Bay. As critical infrastructure, lift stations can't go down. When they fail, the consequences are immediately hazardous for people and the environment, not to mention expensive for businesses with fines and downtime.

We learned to work fast, work clean, and get it right the first time.

Business owners and property managers started asking if we could handle their other electrical work. The answer was yes, but we needed the right licensing to do it properly. So in 2026, we launched Purple Current as a full-service commercial electrical contractor.

We bring to Purple Current the same high standards we'd always applied to our happy customers at ACE Septic & Waste. From initial sales and installations to long-term maintenance contracts, we show up on time, communicate clearly, and don't leave until it's done right. Every project, every time.
