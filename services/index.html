Who We Are

Full-Scope Commercial Electrical

Commercial electrical work isn't residential work at scale. The systems are more complex, the code requirements are stricter, and a failed inspection doesn't just cost money. It shuts down your project. Purple Current exists because commercial clients need a contractor who operates at that level every day.

We've spent 15 years doing commercial electrical work in Tampa Bay. Property managers, facilities directors, general contractors, and business owners hire us because we show up prepared, we communicate what's happening on the job, and our work passes inspection the first time. Every job is permitted, inspected, and documented.

Tampa Bay is our core service area, and we respond fast when the situation calls for it. For larger projects, we work across Florida.

Commercial Electrical Installation

New construction and tenant buildouts live and die by the schedule. We handle complete electrical installation from rough-in through trim-out on commercial, industrial, and multi-family projects. That means coordinating with your GC, pulling permits on time, and getting inspections closed without delays that hold up the next trade.

Electrical Maintenance & Repair

Panel Upgrades & Service Upgrades

A panel that can't keep up with your load is a productivity problem before it becomes a safety problem. We run the load calculations, size the upgrade correctly, coordinate with the utility when required, and handle everything from permit through final inspection.

Commercial Lighting Installation

Good lighting spec means nothing if the installation doesn't match. We install and commission commercial interior and exterior lighting systems, including LED conversions, controls integration, and code-compliant fixture layouts. New construction or retrofit, the result is the same: it works on day one and holds up long-term.

Surge Protection

One voltage event can destroy equipment worth more than the protection would have cost. We install commercial-grade surge protection at the service entrance and point of use, sized to the specific load profile and equipment sensitivity of your facility.

Learn more

Generator Installation

Grid failures happen. Your operation doesn't get to stop when they do. We install commercial standby generators and transfer switch systems sized for your critical loads, from small commercial facilities to large industrial applications. We handle the full electrical scope from pad to panel.

Learn more

UPS Installation

The gap between a power failure and generator startup is long enough to crash servers, corrupt data, and interrupt critical processes. We install and integrate commercial UPS systems for data centers, medical facilities, industrial controls, and any operation where a momentary interruption causes real damage.

Learn more

EV Charging Installation

Tenants, employees, and customers increasingly expect EV charging on-site. We design and install Level 2 and DC fast charging systems for commercial properties, office parks, retail centers, and multi-family developments, including the electrical service upgrades that the new infrastructure demands.

Learn more

Emergency Lighting Installation

Emergency lighting and exit signs aren't optional. They are required for occupancy, they are inspected, and they need to work when the power doesn't. We install battery backup units, central inverter systems, and photometric-compliant layouts for commercial and multi-family properties.

Learn more

Parking Lot Lighting

Dark parking lots create liability. We install and service pole-mounted fixtures, LED retrofits, photocell controls, and site distribution systems for retail centers, office parks, warehouses, and multi-family properties. The goal is even coverage that meets code and actually makes the site safe.

Learn more

Sign Wiring

Illuminated signs need clean, code-compliant electrical connections, and the timing has to line up with the sign contractor's schedule. We handle the rough-in and final connections for commercial signage, coordinating directly with sign vendors and property managers so access and power are ready when they need to be.

Learn more

Electrical Retrofit & Renovation

Older facilities carry wiring and panels that were never designed for today's electrical loads. We plan and execute commercial retrofits that bring infrastructure up to current code, improve efficiency, and keep disruption to active operations as short as possible.
