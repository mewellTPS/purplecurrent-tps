Core Services

Purple Current focuses on large-scale commercial projects.
From complex renovations and ongoing maintenance to updating an existing building or planning a new facility from the ground up, our process is built on practical solutions, safety, and efficiency.
  • Commercial electrical installation: From new builds to tenant improvements, we design and install complete commercial power and lighting systems.
  • Maintenance and repair: For service and troubleshooting to keep existing systems safe and functional.
  • Generator installation: Standby and backup generators to keep key operations running during outages.
  • Panel upgrades and replacement: Add capacity, correct hazards, and bring older panels up to current code.
  • Lighting installation: Interior and exterior lighting for offices, warehouses, retail, and common areas.
  • Surge protection: Protect your entire building and help safeguard sensitive equipment and critical systems.
  • UPS systems: Clean, uninterrupted power for servers, workstations, and other essential devices.
  • Emergency lighting and egress lighting: Code-compliant systems that support safe evacuation during power loss.
  • Retrofit and renovation: Electrical upgrades that modernize aging infrastructure in existing buildings.
  • Power distribution and switchgear: Design and installation of main service gear and distribution equipment.
  • Control panels and motor controls: Power and controls for pumps, lift stations, HVAC, and process equipment.

Let our team align our work with your project schedule, operational needs, or facility status. As commercial electrical contractors in Florida with an exclusive emphasis on power, lighting, and controls for commercial environments, our team’s resources are focused on both quick-turn and long-term commercial work for municipal and industrial customers.
